## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is Turn Timer?

Turn Timer is a visual sequence timer for turns, routines, recipes, classrooms,
meetings, plant watering, and other real-world countdowns.

### What is free?

The quick visual timer, starter templates, basic playback, recent history, and
one custom saved template are free.

### What does Turn Timer Pro unlock?

Turn Timer Pro unlocks unlimited saved templates, full history export, iCloud
template sync, sharing/import/export workflows, and favorite-template widgets.

### How do I restore Pro?

Open the Pro screen and choose Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed into
the same Apple account used for the original purchase.

### Why is iCloud sync not updating?

Check:

- You are signed into iCloud on the device.
- iCloud Drive is enabled.
- Turn Timer is allowed to use iCloud in system settings.
- You have unlocked Turn Timer Pro.
- The device has network access.

CloudKit sync can take a short time to settle. If a template still does not
appear, edit or save the template again to queue another sync.

### How do widgets work?

Widgets show your favorite saved template. Tap Start in the widget to open Turn
Timer and start that template in the app.

Widgets are not a background live timer. They are quick-start surfaces.

### The widget says no favorite exists. What should I do?

Open Turn Timer, go to Templates, save or choose a template, and mark it as your
favorite with the star button.

### Does the Apple Watch app sync saved templates?

Not yet. The current watch app uses watch-local starter templates and a simple
crown-adjustable countdown. Saved template sync to watchOS is planned as a
separate future feature.

### How do I share a template?

Open Templates, choose the template, and use the export/share action. Turn Timer
creates a portable `.turntimer` file that another Turn Timer user can import.

### Does importing overwrite my existing templates?

No. Imported templates are duplicated with a new local template ID and a
conflict-safe title.

### How do I delete app data?

Delete individual templates/history in the app where available. To remove all
local Turn Timer data from a device, delete the app from the device. If iCloud
sync is enabled, also manage the app's iCloud data through Apple system
settings.
