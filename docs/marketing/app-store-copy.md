## Description Draft

```text
Turn Timer keeps game nights, routines, and group activities moving with colorful visual countdowns. Create reusable rounds for players, recipe steps, classroom stations, meeting speakers, plant watering zones, focus blocks, and more.

KEEP TURNS FAIR

Set up a sequence once, then run it whenever you need it. Each round gets a name, color, sound, and duration so everyone can see whose turn or step is active.

BUILT FOR REAL LIFE

- Start a quick visual timer with one tap
- Use starter templates for game night, recipes, plants, classrooms, and meetings
- Build custom templates with named rounds and repeats
- Skip, restart, pause, do over, or add a round during a session
- Review recent session history after a timer completes
- Import and export portable Turn Timer template files

SAVE THE SETUPS YOU REUSE

Turn Timer is strongest when you have a timer path you run again and again: a board game turn order, a recipe process, a class rotation, a meeting agenda, a focus routine, or a watering route.

TURN TIMER PRO

Turn Timer is free for quick timers, starter templates, basic playback, recent history, and one custom saved template. Turn Timer Pro unlocks reuse and portability features such as unlimited saved templates, full history export, iCloud sync, sharing, and widgets where available in the submitted build.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

Turn Timer does not use third-party tracking, ads, or analytics SDKs. Your templates and history stay on your device unless you use iCloud sync through your Apple account.

Download Turn Timer and make time visible for the people, steps, and routines that matter.
```
