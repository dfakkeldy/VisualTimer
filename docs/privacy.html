Privacy Policy

Turn Timer is designed to work without developer-run accounts, ads, tracking, or analytics SDKs.

Developer

Turn Timer is developed by Kinnoki Labs. For privacy questions, contact dfakkeldy@gmail.com.

Information Stored By The App

Turn Timer stores the information needed to run timers and reusable sequences on your device, including timer duration and sound preferences, saved templates, round names, colors, sounds, durations, repeat settings, local session history, and purchase entitlement state.

Information Collected By The Developer

Kinnoki Labs does not collect timer templates, session history, device analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking data from Turn Timer. The app does not include third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

iCloud Sync

If an installed build includes iCloud sync and you choose to use it, Turn Timer stores supported templates or history in your private iCloud database through Apple's CloudKit service. Kinnoki Labs does not operate a separate account server for this sync and cannot read the contents of your private iCloud database.

App Store Purchases

Turn Timer Pro purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit and the App Store. Kinnoki Labs does not receive your full payment card details.

Support Requests

If you contact support, Kinnoki Labs receives the email address and message content you choose to send. Support messages are used only to respond to your request and manage support.

Information Not Accessed

Turn Timer does not currently access location, contacts, camera, photos, microphone, HealthKit, motion data, or browsing history.

Sharing

Kinnoki Labs does not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Data may be shared only with Apple services you choose to use, when you intentionally export or share a Turn Timer template file, when required by law, or to respond to a support request you initiated.

Retention And Deletion

Local templates and history remain on your device until you delete them or delete the app. iCloud-synced data remains in your private iCloud database according to Apple iCloud behavior and your iCloud settings. Support messages are retained only as long as reasonably needed to answer and manage support.

Children's Privacy

Turn Timer is not directed to children under 13. Kinnoki Labs does not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app.

Changes

This policy may be updated when Turn Timer's features or data practices change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
